About MDL

Our team of medical professionals and laboratory technicians are dedicated to providing the highest quality of care. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we ensure accurate diagnoses and timely results for our clients.

  • Leading Regional Dermatopathology lab based in Rochester, NY
  • Founded in 1984 by Dr. Jan Muhlbauer
  • Currently serving Upstate NY with over 50 clients
  • Process & read over 50,000 patient specimens annually
  • CLIA Licensed & NYS Permitted, including Histotechs

Reporting Services

We offer comprehensive reporting services including EMR integration and automatic remote printing capabilities. Our team strives to provide the quickest turnaround times in the industry while maintaining the highest standards of diagnostic accuracy.

Specimen Analysis

Our expert staff of dermatopathologists brings decades of combined experience to every analysis. We utilize advanced diagnostic techniques to ensure accurate and timely results for every specimen we receive.

Family-Owned Practice

Unlike large corporate laboratories, we're a family-owned and operated practice deeply rooted in the Rochester community since 1984. All processing and diagnostics are performed on-site at our Pittsford facility, allowing for direct physician-to-physician communication and a personalized approach to each case.
