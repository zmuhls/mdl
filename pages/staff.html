Our Staff

Meet our team of expert dermathologists, lab technicians, and administrative staff

Our team of medical professionals and laboratory technicians are dedicated to providing the highest quality of care. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we ensure accurate diagnoses and timely results for our clients.

About MDL

  • Leading Regional Dermatopathology lab based in Rochester, NY
  • Founded in 1984 by Dr. Jan Muhlbauer and currently staffed with 3 dermatopathologists
  • Currently serving Upstate NY with over 50 clients
  • Process & read over 70,000 patient specimens annually
  • CLIA Licensed & NYS Permitted, including Histotechs

Our Mission is to assure the best healthcare for every patient by providing expert, accurate and prompt interpretation of skin biopsies for our referring providers.
