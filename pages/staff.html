Dr. Jan E. Muhlbauer, M.D.

Dr. Muhlbauer founded MDL in 1984 and has served as its President and Medical Director ever since. With over three decades of experience in dermatopathology, he has established MDL as a leader in the field, known for both diagnostic excellence and exceptional service.

Professional Background

  • Founded Muhlbauer Dermatopathology Laboratory in 1984
  • President of Muhlbauer Dermatopathology Laboratory
  • Graduated from Southwestern Medical School in 1976
  • Completed Residency in Dermatology and Fellowship in Dermatopathology at Harvard
  • Board Certified in Dermatology (1980) and Dermatopathology (1982)
  • 36 years of experience in Clinical Dermatology
  • 34 years of experience in Dermatopathology
  • Clinical Assistant Professor of Dermatology at the University of Rochester
  • Consulting pathologist, ViaHealth, Rochester General Hospital
  • 24 years of experience as MDL medical director

Achievements and Specialties

Dr. Muhlbauer has contributed significantly to the field of dermatopathology throughout his career. His areas of special interest include melanocytic lesions, cutaneous lymphomas, and inflammatory dermatoses. He has published numerous articles in peer-reviewed journals and frequently lectures at national and international dermatopathology conferences. Under his leadership, MDL has grown from a small local laboratory to a respected regional dermatopathology reference center, serving hundreds of dermatologists and their patients throughout the Northeast.

Our Laboratory Team

Our laboratory is staffed by experienced, certified technicians who are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality and accuracy. Each team member brings specialized expertise that ensures specimens are processed with the utmost care and precision.

From histotechnologists to laboratory assistants, our team works collaboratively to support our pathologists in delivering timely and accurate diagnoses.
