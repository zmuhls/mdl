Specimen Processing & Interpretation

We collaborate with dermatology practices to ensure accurate and timely specimen analysis. Our experienced team provides expert histopathologic interpretations with exceptional turnaround times.

  • Complete histopathologic examination
  • Special stain techniques as needed
  • Immunohistochemistry studies
  • Digital pathology capabilities
  • Consultations on challenging cases

Insurance Services

  • Direct billing to all major insurance carriers

  • Patient billing support

Courier Services

Our dedicated courier service offers daily specimen pickup and report delivery to streamline your practice's workflow.

  • Specialized handling and safe transport

EMR Integration

Our lab is fully compatible with multiple EMR systems, including Modernizing Medicine and Medent. This allows for seamless report access and efficient patient record management.

  • Bi-directional EMR interfaces
  • Secure electronic report delivery
  • Automated result uploads
  • Compatible with major dermatology EMR systems
  • Remote printing capabilities
