Specimen Processing & Interpretation

We collaborate with dermatology practices to ensure accurate and timely specimen analysis. Our experienced team provides expert histopathologic interpretations with exceptional turnaround times.

  • Complete histopathologic examination
  • Special stain techniques as needed
  • Immunohistochemistry studies
  • Digital pathology capabilities
  • Consultations on challenging cases

Insurance Services & Billing

We work with all insurance providers to ensure seamless billing for your patients with minimal administrative burden on your practice.

  • Direct billing to all major insurance carriers including Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial plans
  • Patient-friendly billing with simplified explanation of benefits
  • Transparent pricing and claims handling
  • Dedicated billing specialists available to assist patients with questions
  • Electronic claims submission for faster processing
  • Coordination with secondary insurance when applicable

Courier Services

Our dedicated courier service offers reliable specimen transportation with flexible scheduling options to streamline your practice's workflow.

  • Scheduled courier service for specimen pickup with flexible timing options
  • Prompt and reliable specimen transport with tracking
  • Regular delivery of specimen collection supplies to your office
  • Temperature-controlled transportation to maintain specimen integrity
  • Specialized handling for rush or priority specimens
  • Convenient report delivery options including electronic, fax, or courier

EMR Integration & Reporting

Our lab is fully compatible with multiple EMR systems, allowing for seamless report access and efficient patient record management.

  • EMR interface with several leading systems including Medent, Modernizing Medicine (EMA), EZDerm, and EClinicals
  • Secure, easy-to-use portal access for your practice
  • Automatic remote printing capabilities
  • Fax report delivery when needed
  • Custom reporting formats available upon request
  • Bi-directional EMR interfaces
  • Automated result uploads
